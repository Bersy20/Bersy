AUTOMATIC HEALTH SURVEILLANCE SYSTEM
Location: Embedded project expo, Panimalar engineering college, 19th September 2020.

Description:  This project is designed to continuously monitor the heart rate and temperature of the patients and in case of any abnormal condition the alarm sounds and the alert message is sent to the care taker through the GSM module. It also monitors the presence of harmful gases like CO and combustible gases. If it detects it sends alert message to caretaker and alarm sounds URL: https://youtu.be/U0TEQ1iUQJ8
ULTRASONIC BLIND STICK WITH GPS TRACKING
Location: Science bazaar, Panimalar engineering college, 6th March 2020.

Description:  This project is designed with ultrasonic sensor-based a ssistance blind stick. GPS tracking is integrated together in this design for the assistance of care taker. The use of microcontroller will help the visually impaired people to overcome the physical challenges.
