Paper Presentation

ROLE OF VIRTUAL REALITY IN LIFE OF DISABLED AND IN SELLING PRODUCTS
Conference: 3rd International Conference on Emerging Current Trends in COMET Panimalar Engineering College, on 6th March 2020.

Description:  Virtual Reality gives the potential for physically disabled to try out of reach experiences such as climbing mountains, skateboarding or swimming in the sea ,perhaps for the first time. It also helps disabled people to recover from stroke, sports injury or vestibular system issues by improving motor skills and aiding muscle recovery. The people with autism disability can also use VR for safety purpose. The customers can virtually experience the products using VR. It can supplement the bright colourful images, detailed product descriptions and reviews that customers are currently using to make a buying decision online.
